About Losty


Traveling can often lead to misplaced luggage, causing inconvenience and stress. The QR Luggage Tracker aims to mitigate this issue by providing travelers with a unique QR code that, when scanned, displays the owner’s contact information, making it easier for lost luggage to be returned.

Losty is the QR Luggage ( items ) Tracker is a web application designed to help travelers easily recover lost luggage. Upon registering, users receive a unique QR code that can be attached to their luggage. If the luggage is lost and later found, anyone who scans the QR code can see the registered owner’s name and email, facilitating the return of the lost item.

Steps to use Losty


  1. In the Genreate QR section you need to enter your details which you wanted to share to the who finds and scan your QR code.

  2. After filling your details you just need to click on the Generate QR Code icon.

  3. When the QR code is generated you can Download the Code in the form of png image.

  4. The final step is Paste this QR Code on your luggage or your items.
